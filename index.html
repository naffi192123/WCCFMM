OUR RESEARCH FOCUS

Pioneering Research Areas for Transformative Healthcare

1

Cancer Biology

Understanding the fundamental mechanisms of cancer development to inform prevention, diagnosis, and treatment strategies.

2

Cancer Therapeutics

Developing innovative therapies to effectively target and treat various forms of cancer.

3

Drug Discovery

Identifying and developing new drugs to treat cancer and other diseases through cutting-edge research and technology.

4

Biomarker Discovery

Finding biological markers that can be used for early detection, diagnosis, and monitoring of diseases.

5

Genome Instability

Investigating the causes and consequences of genome instability to understand its role in cancer and other diseases.

6

Gene Regulation

Exploring how genes are turned on and off in cells and understanding their role in health and disease.
